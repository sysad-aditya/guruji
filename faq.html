Frequently Asked Questions

What is Vedic Astrology?

Vedic astrology is an ancient system of astrology that originated in India thousands of years ago. It provides insights into your life, personality, relationships, career, and much more by analyzing the position of planets at the time of your birth.

What is KP astrology?

KP astrology is a predictive system that offers accurate insights into specific life events based on the unique Krishnamurti Paddhati (K.P.) methodology. It allows for precise predictions about matters such as marriage, career, health, and more.

Yes, our birth chart analysis is based on traditional Vedic astrology principles and is highly accurate. Guru Ji provides personalized insights into your life based on your unique planetary positions at birth.
