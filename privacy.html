We collect personal information such as your full name, date of birth, time of birth, place of birth, and payment details when you request a birth chart or use our services. We may also collect your email address for communication purposes.

Your personal information is used solely for providing personalized astrology services, including generating and sending your birth chart analysis. We will also use your contact details to communicate with you regarding your request, payment, and other service-related matters.

3. Payment Information

All payment transactions are processed securely through third-party payment providers. We do not store any sensitive payment information such as credit card numbers or banking details on our servers. Payment information is used exclusively for processing your transaction.

We employ a variety of security measures to ensure the safety of your personal information. All sensitive data, including payment details, is encrypted and transmitted securely. We also restrict access to your personal information to authorized personnel only.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. If you wish to request a change to your data or if you have any concerns about your information, please contact us using the contact details provided on this website.
